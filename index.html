About Me

Hi! I'm an enthusiast from McMaster University who is pursuing a degree in Mechatronics Engineering. Some of my favorite things include coding, robots, circuits, UI/UX design, Sports and EDM music.


I am currently trying to find a summer internship upto a period of 16 months as of May 2016. My top skills as of now include Java, Python, C/C++, JavaScript and Arduino. I am also really passionate about drones and so far have made one autonomous fighting robot and a quadcopter using Arduino. I also possess familiarity with HTML/CSS, PHP, JQuery, SQL and PCB Circuit Design.
